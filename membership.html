Membership

The Society of Mechanical and Automotive Engineers exists to promote excellence in research and education in the field of Mechanical and Automobile Engineering. The membership provides support and information for you during your career in the field of Mechanical and Automobile. You can join at any time of the year and at any stage of your career.

Student Membership Guidelines

The Member must be pursuing education in the fields of Mechanical and Automobile from an AICTE recognized organization. This membership is valid up to the Graduation. Our Executive council has right to refuse the application and right to cancel the membership.

Guidlines

  • The Member must be pursuing education in the fields of Mechanical and Automobile from a AICTE recognized organization.
  • This membership is valid up to the Graduation.
  • Our Executive council has right to refuse the application and right to cancel the membership.

Benefits

  • As a student member, member will gain skills and practical experience outside the classroom.
  • Member can publish Technical articles in SMAE publications.
  • Member can discuss his doubts in SMAE forums.
  • Member will get certificate from SMAE.

Life Membership Guidelines

Member must hold a Degree. Member must be pursuing his professional career in the related fields of Mechanical and Automobile. Our Executive council has right to refuse the application and right to cancel the membership.

Guidlines

  • Member must hold a Degree.
  • Member must be pursuing his professional career in the related fields of Mechanical and Automobile.
  • Our Executive council has right to refuse the application and right to cancel the membership.

Benefits

  • Member can discuss his doubts in SMAE forums.
  • Member can publish Technical articles in SMAE publications.
  • Member has benefit of 50% fee waiver in workshops and Conferences conducted by SMAE.
  • Member will receive Monthly Newsletter.
  • Member may have opportunity to become a member in SMAE committee.
