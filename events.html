Technical Workshops

SMAE organized Workshop on “Design and Fabrication of a Go-Kart” was Hosted by Sri Indu College of Engineering and Technology, Hyderabad where 150+ Participants witnessed the Workshop throughout India.

SMAE organized Official Workshop of Students Kart Design Challenge SEASON-II from June 9th-10th, 2018, Hosted by Guru Nanak Institute of Technology, Hyderabad throughout india 200+ participants witnessed for this 2-Day Official Workshop, students underwent into different topics some of them are Engine & Transmission, Braking, Steering, Importance of Safety of Drivers and Vehicle Design approach, Design validation plan, Failure mode effect analysis, Entrepreneurship Plan, Cost report, Design report.
